Privacy Policy

Principia — Axiom 38 LLC · Effective March 1, 2026

1. Introduction and Identity of Data Controller

Axiom 38 LLC ("we," "us," or "our") is a Michigan limited liability company and the developer and operator of Principia (bundle ID: com.axiom38.principia), a mathematics education application available for iOS and Android devices (the "App" or "Service").

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect from users of the App, how we use it, how we protect it, and what rights you have regarding your information.

Axiom 38 LLC is the data controller responsible for the personal information described in this policy. We are not a data broker, advertiser, or marketing company. We are an educational technology company. Our purpose in collecting any data is solely to provide and improve the App.

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at support@getprincipia.com.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the App, including:

  • Adults using the App for personal learning;
  • Parents or guardians managing accounts for children under 13;
  • Child users (under 13) for whom a parent or guardian has provided verifiable parental consent.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide the Service. Specifically, we may collect:

(a) Learning Progress Data
We collect data about your activity within the App, including which topics or modules you have completed, quiz and practice results, mastery levels, time spent on activities, and similar in-app learning metrics. This data is used to personalize your experience and allow you to track your progress.

(b) Account Information
If you create an account, we collect your email address. We do not collect your full name unless you voluntarily provide it as part of your display name.

(c) Device Information
We may collect non-personally identifiable device information such as device type, operating system version, App version, and crash/diagnostic data to help us maintain and improve the App. This information does not identify you personally.

(d) Purchase Status (via RevenueCat)
We use RevenueCat, Inc. to manage subscription entitlements. RevenueCat provides us with information about whether your account has an active subscription and what tier of access you have. We do not receive your full credit card or payment details. All payment information is handled directly by Apple or Google.

(e) Parental Email (for Child Users Under 13 Only)
When setting up an account for a child under 13, we collect the parent's or guardian's email address for the purpose of providing verifiable parental consent under the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). This email is used solely for consent verification and communications about the child's account. See Section 5 for more information.

Information We Do NOT Collect: We do not collect your full legal name, precise or approximate geographic location, device contacts, camera or microphone access, photos or video, biometric data, browsing history outside the App, advertising identifiers for advertising purposes, Social Security numbers, government ID numbers, or financial account information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing the Service: To operate, maintain, and deliver the features and functionality of the App, including displaying your learning progress and syncing your data across devices (for paid subscribers).
  • Personalization: To tailor the App experience to your progress level and learning history.
  • Account Management: To create and manage your account, verify your identity, and communicate with you about your account.
  • Subscription Management: To verify your subscription status through RevenueCat and provide access to premium features accordingly.
  • App Improvement: To analyze aggregate, anonymized usage patterns to identify bugs, improve content, and enhance the user experience.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide technical assistance.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations, including COPPA requirements for child users.
  • Safety and Security: To detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, or security vulnerabilities.

We do not use your information for behavioral advertising, retargeting, profiling for commercial purposes, or any purpose not listed above.

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Service Providers

We share limited data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the Service:

  • RevenueCat, Inc. — Subscription management, entitlement verification, and purchase validation. Data shared: account identifiers and purchase records.
  • Supabase, Inc. — Cloud database and synchronization services for paid subscribers only. Data shared: learning progress data, account identifiers. Cloud sync is only activated for users with an active paid subscription.

4.2 No Advertising Networks

We do not share your personal information with any advertising network, data broker, or marketing platform. We do not serve any advertisements within the App.

4.3 No Sale of Personal Information

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to any third party for their own commercial purposes. We will never sell your data.

4.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect the rights or property of Axiom 38 LLC, prevent wrongdoing, or protect the personal safety of users.

4.5 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you prior to your information becoming subject to a materially different privacy policy.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

We are committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and to protecting the privacy of children under the age of 13.

Verifiable Parental Consent: We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. A parent or guardian must provide their email address and affirmatively check the consent acknowledgment box in the App.

What We Collect from Child Users: For children under 13, we collect only learning progress data and the parent's or guardian's email address for consent purposes. We do not collect a child's name, location, photo, or any contact information from the child directly.

Parental Rights: Parents and legal guardians may at any time review their child's data, request deletion, withdraw consent, or refuse further collection by contacting support@getprincipia.com.

For complete details, see our COPPA Notice for Parents and Guardians.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within 30 days, except where required by law to retain certain records longer.

Data associated with child accounts under 13 will be deleted promptly upon receipt of a parental deletion request, withdrawal of consent, or account deletion.

We may retain anonymized, aggregated data indefinitely for purposes of product development and improvement.

7. Security

We take reasonable and appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using TLS/HTTPS protocols;
  • Encryption of data at rest in our cloud infrastructure (Supabase);
  • Access controls limiting employee and contractor access to personal data on a need-to-know basis;
  • Regular review of our security practices.

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

8. Your Rights

Regardless of where you live, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: Request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Right to Deletion: Request that we delete your personal information.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on your consent, withdraw that consent at any time.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your personal data in a portable, machine-readable format.

To exercise any of these rights, contact support@getprincipia.com. We will respond within 30 days.

9. California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you may have additional rights under the CCPA and CPRA, including the right to know what personal information we collect, the right to delete, the right to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information (we do not sell or share personal information), and the right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights.

To submit a CCPA/CPRA request, contact support@getprincipia.com. We will respond within 45 days.

10. International Users

The App is operated in the United States. If you are accessing the App from outside the United States, your information will be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States. By using the App from outside the United States, you consent to the transfer, storage, and processing of your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

If you are located in the EEA, the UK, or Switzerland, you may have rights under the GDPR or equivalent legislation. To exercise those rights, contact support@getprincipia.com.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will take effect no sooner than 30 days after notification. For changes that affect children's data, we will seek renewed parental consent where required by COPPA.
